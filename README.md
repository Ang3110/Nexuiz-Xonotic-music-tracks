# Xonotic + Nexuiz Music Integration

### About This Mod
In this pack, you can experience both classic and contemporary tracks within your game, giving you the best of both worlds.

---

### Installation Instructions
1.  Warning: Before proceeding, navigate to your Xonotic "data/" folder and locate the original "xonotic-20230620-music.pk3". Create a copy of this file and store it in a safe location outside of your Xonotic directory or rename it. This ensures you can easily revert to the original settings if needed.
2.  Install the Mod: paste the data folder into your Xonotic directory. 
3.  Merge: When prompted by your operating system, choose to Merge/Replace the files.

### How to Manage Your Tracks
Once installed, your new music library will be integrated directly into the game. You can manage, toggle, or remove individual tracks at any time by:
* Navigating to the Multiplayer menu, media, then music player and the new list of tracks shows up.
* Selecting your preferred audio tracks from the list or shuffle them and press play before going in game (you must do this every time you boot the game).
